Who We Are

SCCI is a typical simulation of the outside real life. A one eventful experience that will stay in your heart & you'll watch it in your personality development.

"Inside each and every one of us, deep inside the core of each one's being is a winner waiting to be awakened and unleashed upon the world" and that is what SCCi is all about, unleashing your potentials."

An A+ student can be very high skilled technically during college time yet the scenario that usually takes place is that he doesn't find a place among others in the outside competitive job market,that's why we're here!

ABOUT

Our Aim

  • Our main purpose behind the creation of SCCI since the very beginning, which was bridging the gap between the technical life and the practical life in the market place. SCCI provides its participants with the technical skills and soft skills required in the form of sessions given throughout the year.
  • SCCI will run as a set of workshops concluded by a three days conference. The workshops will span eight to nine weeks period (once a week) to give students trainees (Participants) the chance to digest information and for student instructors to prepare instructional material.
